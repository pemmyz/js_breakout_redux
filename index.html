Controls:

Gamepad Supported: Use Left Stick or D-Pad to move. 'A' toggles auto-follow, 'B' teleports, 'Start' for new game, LB/RB to change speed.

Left/Right Arrows (or Tap Screen Sides): Move Paddle
Up/Down Arrows: Change Ball Speed

Auto-Follow: OFF
